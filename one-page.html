All new athletes are required to start with 10 personal training sessions before moving into our FitCross classes. Sessions include learning over 30 FitCross movements, skill development, conditioning, mobility, nutrition, and online workout tracking.

FitCross Moms

Specifically for Moms with newborns or children up to the age of 3. These classes offer a flexible, like minded environment for busy moms who need to have their children at the gym while they train. Moms work to get their post-baby fitness back by focusing on cardio conditioning and building basic strength.
